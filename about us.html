Dev Shukla also won The Best Speaker Award To salute the great souls of martyrs who fought valiantly in the Kargil War, the 20th anniversary of Kargil Vijaya Diwas was celebrated on 26th July. Sub Maj Kaushal Kumar Sharma, SM, one of the Kargil war hero graced the occasion and inspired the students with his experiences and encouraging words. The programme included a heartrending speech by Khushpreet of class X A, a thought provoking poem by Anand of class V-B, Patriotic Group Song and Hindi Role-Play which filled the air with the patriotic fervor. APS, Ferozepur observed the 'International Day Against Drug Abuse and Illicit Trafficking' for which the following activities were organized. Students, Teaching, Non-Teaching and Group D Staff wore badges with Anti Drug Abuse slogans. Special assembly for classes VI to XII was conducted during which a video on drug abuse was shown, speeches were delivered, poems were recited and a role play on 'Drugs - The Monsters' was performed. Lastly, the entire gathering of approximately - 825 individuals took the pledge to live a drug free life. An informative presentation on harmful effects of drugs was shown to the students of classes IX to XII. A poster making competition on the cause was also organised.
