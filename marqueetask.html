INTERNATIONAL ASTEROID DAY

Asteroid Day is an annual global event held on June 30th to raise awareness about asteroids and the potential hazards they pose to Earth. The event aims to educate people about asteroids, their scientific importance, and the need for increased efforts in asteroid discovery, tracking, and planetary defense.
